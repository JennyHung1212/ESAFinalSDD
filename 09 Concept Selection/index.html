We set “Singapore Challenge” (one of our generated concepts) as our datum with a total weighted score of 3. As a group, we scored the different concepts based on the concept sketches, as well as short write-ups we did for each of our concepts.

We eventually downselected our final concept sketch, “PenPla”, which had the highest total weighted score.

However, while it scored higher than the rest in all attributes, we felt that the differences between “PenPla”’s score and the others were not significant enough i.e. there remained room for improvement. We hence decided to focus our efforts on increasing the quality and variety of content, as well as the degree of fun (since they carry the heaviest weights).
