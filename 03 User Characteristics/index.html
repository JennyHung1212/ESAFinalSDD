User Characteristics

Personas

13-20 years old

Justin Lee is a 16 year old high school student. His hobbies include playing both computer and card games with his friends. As he is still a student, he seldom travels and has little knowledge about Singapore apart from the brief exposure he receives in Geography lessons. However, he is curious about the world and looks forward to exploring the world when he is older.

21-30 years old

A 26 year old female, Michelle Wu works in the finance industry. Due to the nature of her job, she frequently visits other countries on work trips. However, she is often busy and hence does not always get the time to explore the country. Her favourite time of the year is summer and hence her ideal getaway involves going to someplace sunny!
