One player answers in place of the penalized player in the penalty stage

Description

The penalized player (Player 1) draws a penalty card and reads it out. Overly-excited, another player (Player 2) shouts out the answer even though he/ she is not supposed to. The penalty card is thus passed from the penalized player to the player who shouted out the answer.

Initial State

Players are in the midst of the penalty stage. Player 1 lost in the playing stage and has drawn a penalty card (specifically, a challenge penalty card).
