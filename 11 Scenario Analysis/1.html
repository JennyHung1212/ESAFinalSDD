Local playing card appears in playing stage

Description

A player (Player 2) randomly flips open a local playing card and all players respond to its appearance. Players try to execute the corresponding action as fast as possible, but ultimately one player (Player 4) ends up doing it last. The player then gets penalized.

Initial State

Players are in the midst of a playing stage, and Player 1 is due to go next.
