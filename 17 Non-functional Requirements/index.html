Non-functional Requirements

  1. All the cards shall be water-resistant, such that it does not curl up even if dipped in water for 5 seconds and then dried

  2. The cards shall be small enough to hold comfortably in one hand, with dimensions of all cards not exceeding 70mm x 100mm

  3. The length of the entire game (including set-up) for four players shall not exceed 25 minutes, with 90% confidence based on trialling experience and calculations

  4. Prototyping costs (from the first prototype to the final one) shall not exceed SGD180

  5. The quantity of penalty cards shall be sufficient (30 cards) such that chances of the penalty cards running out before the playing cards run out are lower than 5%, based on calculations done
