Design Teams and Design Rules

Logic designers
  • Come up with rules of the game
  • Ensure that the game flows smoothly (e.g. sufficient cards to meet each objective)
  • Decide on what artifacts are needed, the quantities required for each of these artifacts, as well as the content of the game
Artifact designers
  • Do the physical artifacts and packaging designs, as well as the layout of content within each
  • Decide what dimensions the artifacts and packaging should take
Artistic designer
  • Design all images/ graphics needed (for artifacts and packaging)
