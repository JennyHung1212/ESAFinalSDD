Player receives fate penalty card in the penalty stage

Description

A player (Player 4) randomly receives a fate penalty card in the penalty stage, as he/ she was the last to complete an action in the playing stage. The player (Player 4) reads the situation written on the fate penalty card and has to keep it. The penalty stage ends when the penalty card has been kept by a player.

Initial State

The penalty stage has just started. Player 4 is due to receive a penalty card because he/ she was the last to complete an action in the playing stage.
